How Can You Enjoy Your Day In ADRENALINELAND ?

Carnival Food

What's a carnival without classic carnival food! Stop by our multiple vendors to satisfy your cravings, with everything from warm funnel cakes,to freshly made corn dogs! We also offer a variety of benches and picnic areas to rest tired feet,and enjoy your tasty treat!



Games

That coveted giant stuffed teddy bear is waiting for you! Stop by our various classic carnival game booths and try your luck at skee ball, the rising waters, the balloon pop and many more!



Spectacular Rides

For all you thrill seekers and adrenaline junkies, our Spectacular Rides are a must! Whether you're taking a ride upside down and backwards on our one-of-a-kind Delusion or spinning into outer space all! To top it off, our rides change between 250 different colors throughout your ride creating a spectacular vision you won't forget!
